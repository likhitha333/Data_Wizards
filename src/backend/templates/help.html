Help and Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up?

Click on the "Sign Up" button on the home page and fill out the form with your details.

How do I log in?

Click on the "Login" button and enter your registered email and password.

What should I do if I forget my password?

Click on the "Forget Password?" link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.

How do I track my water intake?

Log in to your account and navigate to the home page. Use the "Log Your Water Intake" section to enter your daily intake.

How do I update my account information?

Go to the "Account" page to view and update your personal details.
